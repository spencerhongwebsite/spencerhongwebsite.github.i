Welcome!

I am currently a Ryan Fellow at the International Institute of Nanotechnology and a PhD student studying chemical and biological engineering at Northwestern University. I earned my B.S. in chemical and biomolecular engineering at Cornell University. I work at the intersection between artificial intelligence and language.

Data Analytics

I can deliver concise yet powerful conclusions from big data to help inform your decision-making. In the fields of bioinformatics/cheminformatics, I have helped government contractors and private individuals make the best decisions available.

Simulation & Modeling

Complex chemical phenomena may be difficult to study "on a bench top". I use in silico tools with parallel computing to simulate the chemistry in question. These methods can often explore parts of research that may be extremely difficult or expensive to study in a traditional lab.

Artificial Intelligence

I can develop machine learning models to predict future outcomes from the past to aid your business and/or research decisions. On the artistic side, I can also deliver computational art that is created 100% by artificial intelligence.
